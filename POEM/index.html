enough.

when you start to feel you will never be good enough perphaps, there is room for this moment to be enough: enough room to breathe enough room to think enough room to laugh at the most ridiculous, silly thing enough room to listen to something overheard and contemplate the meaning and why you think they chose that word. enough room to look around a bustling crowded the room and dream for other people what good things they must do; ten minutes from now, ten years from now, imagine the very best for them. even if they are strangers. take a simple, mindful, moment for them. even when you feel you will never be good enough keep your eyes wide open keep your heart filled with love, and whatever is right and whatever is true let that be enough to carry you through, when you start to feel you will never be good enough. Morgan Harper Nichols
